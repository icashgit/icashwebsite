Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an iCASH account?

Download the iCASH app and follow the on-screen instructions to sign up. You'll need to provide some basic information and complete a verification process.

Is iCASH safe to use?

Yes, iCASH uses advanced security measures to protect your data and transactions.

Can I use iCASH without a bank account?

Yes, iCASH can be used without a bank account. You can top up your wallet through various means including over-the-counter services.

How can I update my personal information in iCASH?

Log in to your account, go to 'Account Settings', and choose the information you wish to update.

What should I do if I forget my iCASH PIN?

In the app, select 'Forgot PIN' and follow the steps to reset it. You'll need to verify your identity for security purposes.

What documents are required for iCASH account verification?

Typically, a government-issued ID and proof of address are required. Acceptable documents include a driver’s license, passport, or utility bill.

 What should I do if my verification is declined?

Check if the uploaded documents are clear and valid. If the issue persists, contact our support team for guidance.

What should I do if my verification status is 'Under Review'?

If your verification status in iCASH remains 'Under Review' for an extended period, it's recommended to contact our support team for an update. Sometimes verifications may require additional checks or information. Our team will assist you in understanding the current status of your verification and provide guidance on any further steps required.

How do I send money using iCASH?

Go to the 'Send Money' option, enter the recipient's details, and the amount. Confirm the transaction with your PIN.

Are there any limits on transactions?

Yes, transaction limits vary. Please refer to the iCASH Quick Reference Guide (QRG) for detailed information.

Are there any monthly fees for using iCASH?

iCASH does not charge monthly fees. Transaction fees, if any, are per transaction basis.

What devices are compatible with the iCASH app?

iCASH is compatible with most iOS and Android devices.

How do I update the iCASH app?

Regular updates are released on the App Store and Google Play Store. Ensure your app is updated to the latest version for optimal performance.

Can I suggest improvements or features for iCASH?

Absolutely! We value user feedback. Please email us your suggestions.

What should I do if the iCASH app is not working?

Check your internet connection first. If the problem persists, contact our support team for assistance.

How can I report a suspicious transaction or security concern?

Immediately contact our support team with details of the transaction.
